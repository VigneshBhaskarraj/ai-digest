🏛 Policy & Incentives 3 / 11
Tamil Nadu Government Pursues DRDO Airborne Systems Centre for Hosur

The Tamil Nadu government is continuing active efforts to bring a DRDO Airborne Systems Centre to Hosur, a strategic industrial belt in Krishnagiri district. This would position Hosur as a defense-aerospace R&D node alongside its existing EV manufacturing base. If successful, it would create significant procurement, supply chain, and deep-tech spinoff opportunities for TN startups.

TNERC Approves ₹1,545-Crore Provisional Subsidy for 200 Units Free Power Scheme

Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission has approved a ₹1,545-crore provisional subsidy for the state's 200 units free power scheme. While primarily a welfare measure, the scale of power subsidy signals TN's commitment to affordable electricity infrastructure, which indirectly benefits energy-intensive manufacturing startups and EV charging ecosystem players in the state.

Puducherry Notifies Two New Special Economic Zones including IT/ITES SEZ

The Central Government has notified two new SEZs in Puducherry — one being an IT/ITES SEZ to be developed by Oulgaret Municipality at Thattanchavady village. Puducherry's proximity to Chennai makes this relevant for the broader TN-Puducherry tech corridor, offering startups and GCC players an alternative low-cost jurisdiction with SEZ tax benefits.

Uncertainty around NEET is reportedly fueling a significant rise in engineering college admissions across Tamil Nadu. This structural shift could strengthen TN's already deep engineering talent pipeline, providing more graduates for the state's growing semiconductor, AI, and deep-tech sectors over the next 4–5 years.

📍 District Pulse 6 / 11
Hosur / Krishnagiri Defense-Tech / Aerospace / EV Manufacturing

Tamil Nadu is actively lobbying to establish a DRDO Airborne Systems Centre in Hosur, which already hosts major EV players like Ola Electric and Ultraviolette Automotive. This dual convergence of defense R&D intent and EV manufacturing makes Hosur the most strategically watched corridor in TN right now.

Founders building in defense electronics, drone components, advanced materials, or EV supply chain should be tracking Hosur for co-location, procurement, and partnership opportunities as this corridor matures.

Chennai AI / Enterprise SaaS / GCC

Chennai's anchor tech companies Zoho and Freshworks are both driving national AI adoption conversations — Zoho on productivity vs. compute costs and Freshworks on mid-market AI waste — signaling that Chennai's enterprise tech ecosystem is maturing into a thought-leadership hub. The new Nissan showroom expansion also signals continued consumer market confidence in Chennai.

AI efficiency tooling, cost optimization SaaS, and enterprise AI governance startups have a direct market in Chennai's dense GCC and mid-market enterprise base.

Puducherry IT / ITES / SEZ

Two newly notified SEZs in Puducherry — including a dedicated IT/ITES zone — extend the Chennai tech corridor southward with favorable tax and regulatory incentives. Puducherry's lower real estate costs and proximity to Chennai make it a viable overflow destination for tech firms and GCCs.

IT services firms, GCC back-offices, and tech startups seeking SEZ benefits without Chennai's cost premium should evaluate Puducherry's new IT/ITES SEZ as a base for operations.
