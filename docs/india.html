AI-Native Lean Teams Becoming Fundable Archetype

Sam Altman's observation that AI enables smaller teams to scale is becoming VC orthodoxy globally. Indian investors are likely to follow, favoring solo founders or 2-3 person AI-first teams that can demonstrate 10x productivity gains. Expect lower seed checks but higher outcome expectations — you'll need to show you can do with 3 people what took 20 in 2023.
